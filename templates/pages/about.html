Our Story

Bridging the Gap Between Patients & Doctors

In Bangladesh, millions struggle to access timely medical care. Rural areas lack specialist doctors, urban hospitals are overcrowded, and a simple doctor's visit can consume an entire day.

Smart Care changes this. With a few taps on your phone, you can consult a certified cardiologist, neurologist, or pediatrician — from home, office, or anywhere across the country.
